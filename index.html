Shopping

Venetian, Las Vegas, NV
This is my favorite place to shop when I am staying in Las Vegas. It is indoors and the air conditioning works great when it is over 100 degress outside. I really like the fake sky that changes brightness throughout the day to simulate a really outdoor experience. You could easily spend an entire day or two in here just looking at the shops.
